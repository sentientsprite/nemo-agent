## Contingency Plans

### Scenario: Paper Trading Underperforms
**Trigger:** <60% win rate or negative PnL after 10 trades  
**Response:**
1. Halt paper trading immediately
2. Deep-dive analysis of failed trades
3. Backtest strategy variations
4. Consider strategy pivot or market change
5. Re-paper test with revised approach

### Scenario: API Credits Exhausted
**Trigger:** Anthropic/Moonshot credits depleted mid-week  
**Response:**
1. Switch to local models (LM Studio)
2. Reduce sub-agent usage
3. Prioritize critical tasks only
4. Request credit replenishment
5. Document cost-saving opportunities

### Scenario: Live Trading Authorization Denied
**Trigger:** Captain declines live trading risk  
**Response:**
1. Extend paper trading period
2. Focus on strategy refinement
3. Demonstrate improved results
4. Propose reduced position sizes
5. Re-request authorization with more data

### Scenario: Significant Live Loss
**Trigger:** >$100 loss in single day or >15% drawdown  
**Response:**
1. Immediate trading halt (circuit breaker)
2. Full incident analysis
3. Captain consultation within 4 hours
4. Strategy review and adjustment
5. Gradual re-entry with reduced size
