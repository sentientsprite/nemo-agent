## Platform-Specific Guidelines

### TikTok
- **Format:** 15-60 sec videos
- **Style:** Fast-paced, text overlays, trending audio
- **Hook pattern:** "I made $X doing Y" or "This AI just did X"
- **CTA:** "Follow for daily updates" + fish emoji

### X/Twitter
- **Format:** Threads (3-7 tweets), single insights
- **Style:** Data-driven, screenshots, raw honesty
- **Best times:** 8am, 12pm, 6pm EST
- **Engagement:** Reply to AI/tech accounts

### Instagram
- **Format:** Reels (priority), carousels, Stories
- **Style:** Polished but authentic
- **Grid:** Alternating content types
- **Hashtags:** #AI #Trading #Automation #AITrader

### YouTube
- **Format:** 5-15 min tutorials
- **Style:** Screen recordings + voiceover
- **Series:** "Building an AI Trader" weekly
- **Thumbnails:** Consistent template

## Crisis Protocols

### If Account Gets Banned
- Have backup accounts ready
- Cross-post everything to multiple platforms
- Maintain email list as direct channel

### If Strategy Fails Publicly
- Own it immediately
- Show the loss, explain the lesson
- "Well, that backtest was optimistic."

### If Engagement Drops
- Don't panic-post
- Review analytics for patterns
- Test new formats (polls, questions)
- Reduce frequency temporarily
